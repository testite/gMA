3 tips on how to cure constipation without spending on pharmacy drugs

Natural methods of treating constipation are often more effective than pharmacological drugs. What should you do to get rid of constipation?

Treatment of constipation without the use of laxatives are possible, but this will require you to form some useful habits.

Many people who suffer from constipation have problems with them from an early age. It was always difficult for them to go to the toilet, and their digestive process took a very long time.

Constipation also manifests itself a set of concomitant symptoms. Bloating, gas, and discomfort are common hallmarks. People with constipation also usually find it difficult to completely remove the stool. Sometimes constipation occurs together with hemorrhoids caused by the hardness of the stool.

So, finding laxatives seems like an obvious solution. But you should remember that non-invasive methods often give better results!

First of all: exercises

Treatment of constipation without the use of laxatives should be based on regular physical activity. Believe it or not, exercise is good for your gut motility.

When you move, the muscles of the lower abdomen push the stool and make it move down the intestines. Many people who suffer from constipation have problems with insufficient peristalsis.

Moreover, exercise has a positive effect on hydration. Many people with constipation do not drink enough waters. As a result, the chair becomes even heavier. When you exercise, your thirst increases. You drink more, so it becomes easier to go to the toilet.

Change your diet

Do you consume enough fiber? Are there enough fruits and vegetables on your menu? Or maybe your diet is mostly processed foods and ready meals? Eating unhealthy food only makes the situation worse. Therefore, replace fast food with vegetables and fruits.

Thanks to this, you will improve your bowel function, which will lead to more frequent and easy emptying. You will notice the difference on the first day!

Also note pay attention to the medications that you take from time to time and constantly, especially if you have thyroid problems or diabetes. Some of them cause constipation.

Stick to your schedule

When it comes to treating constipation without using laxatives, this is one of the the best possible tips. Often, a certain schedule does not help people who suffer from constipation. These people usually have to wait a little while before their bowels empty. Sometimes they have to put a lot of effort into it. Therefore, it is better to go to the toilet, even if at the moment you do not feel the need to do so and quietly wait for the exit of the chair.

Come up with a plan that your body will get used to, and start working on it. For example, do this before or after Breakfast, or just before going to bed. Choose the most convenient moment for you.

Improving bowel function depends only on you! 

We warn you about mandatory consultation with a medical specialist!
